## 10. Optimization Plan

### Optimization Cadence

| Timeframe | Actions |
|-----------|---------|
| Daily | Check spend pacing, pause poor performers |
| Weekly | Review by channel/campaign, adjust bids |
| Bi-weekly | Refresh creative, adjust targeting |
| Monthly | Full review, budget reallocation |

### Optimization Triggers

| Metric | Threshold | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| CPA > target | > 130% | Reduce bids, pause targeting |
| CTR < benchmark | < 50% | Refresh creative |
| Frequency > | > 5 | Expand audience or pause |
| Budget underspent | > 20% | Investigate, reallocate |

### Reallocation Rules

- Move budget from channels with CPA > 150% of target
- Scale channels with CPA < 80% of target (up to 30%)
- Major reallocations (>20%) require approval
